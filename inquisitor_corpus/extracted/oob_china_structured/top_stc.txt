## Subordinate Group Army / Fleet / Bases
- **74th Group Army (第七十四集团军)** [PLAA] (74ga) — type: group_army, HQ: Huizhou, strength: 100%. Formerly 42nd Group Army (Guangzhou MR); redesignated Apr 2017. Contains 2 amphibious brigades with ZTD-05/ZBD-05 systems; probable secondary amphibious force supporting ETC Taiwan ops. [DOD CMPR 2024 p.97] [Wikipedia 74th GA]
- **75th Group Army (第七十五集团军)** [PLAA] (75ga) — type: group_army, HQ: Kunming, strength: 100%. Formerly 41st Group Army (Guangzhou MR); redesignated Apr 2017. Includes mountain combined arms brigade for highland/Vietnam border terrain. [DOD CMPR 2024 p.97] [Wikipedia 75th GA]
- **Southern Theater Command Fleet (South Sea Fleet, 南部战区海军)** [PLAN] (ssf_plan) — type: fleet, HQ: Zhanjiang, strength: 100%. HQ Zhanjiang, Guangdong. Yulin/Sanya bases house SSBNs (Type 094). Home to Shandong (CV-17, commissioned 2019), Fujian (CV-18, commissioned Nov 2025), and 6 marine brigades. Two STCN commanders (Li Pengcheng, Ju Xinchun) reportedly purged/under investigation 2025; replacement status unclear. J-20 at 4th and 5th Bdes; J-16 at 6th and 26th Bdes. Includes 8th Bomber Division (H-6K) at Shaodong/Leiyang. [Heritage Foundation 2026-01-20] [CASI/PLAAF Overview 2023]

Covers southeastern China/South China Sea approaches. 7 brigades: DF-31AG (nuclear), DF-26 (dual), DF-17 (conventional hypersonic), DF-21D (anti-ship), DF-10A (cruise).
