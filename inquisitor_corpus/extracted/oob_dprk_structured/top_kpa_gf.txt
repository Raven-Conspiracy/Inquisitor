# Korean People's Army — General Staff Directorate: KPA Ground Forces (Ryukgun)

**ID**: KPA_GF
**Headquarters**: Pyongyang
**Commander (current)**: —
**Map color code**: #4a7c1a
**Notes**: Largest branch ~1,000,000 active-duty personnel. Organized into 10 regular corps, 2 mechanized corps (806th, 815th), 1 armored corps (820th), 1 artillery corps (620th), Pyongyang Defense Command, Capital Defense Command. >70% of force deployed south of Pyongyang-Wonsan line. Includes ~27 infantry divisions, ~15 armored brigades, 4 mech divisions, ~21 arty brigades per IISS MB 2017. [DIA NKMP 2021; IISS Military Balance 2017 p.304]

## Subordinate Corps / Fleet / Air Division / Missile Bureaus
- **I Corps (1st Corps)** [KPA Ground Forces] (corps_1) — type: corps, HQ: Kumgang County, Kangwon Province, strength: 85.0%. FORWARD CORPS. Eastern DMZ sector. One of four frontline corps facing South Korea. High readiness; believed to include 3-4 infantry divisions + mech/arty/AAA brigades. Exact division designations not confirmed in open sources. [Wikipedia I Corps NK; DIA NKMP 2021; IISS MB 2017]
- **II Corps (2nd Corps)** [KPA Ground Forces] (corps_2) — type: corps, HQ: Wonsan, Kangwon Province (approximate), strength: 82.0%. FORWARD CORPS. Central DMZ sector. One of four frontline corps. Historically had 5 divisions per Bodansky 1984-88. Current sub-unit names unknown in open sources. [Wikipedia KPAGF; DIA NKMP 2021; FAS irp.fas.org]
- **IV Corps (4th Corps)** [KPA Ground Forces] (corps_4) — type: corps, HQ: Sariwon area or Pyongyang region (approximate; sources conflict), strength: 83.0%. FORWARD CORPS. Western DMZ sector facing Seoul axis. One of four highest-readiness ground corps. Specific sub-unit names unconfirmed. [Wikipedia KPAGF; DIA NKMP 2021]
- **V Corps (5th Corps)** [KPA Ground Forces] (corps_5) — type: corps, HQ: Hamhung area or central sector (approximate), strength: 80.0%. FORWARD CORPS. Central/eastern DMZ sector. One of four forward corps. Sub-unit composition unconfirmed in open sources. [Wikipedia KPAGF; DIA NKMP 2021; FAS irp.fas.org]
- **III Corps (3rd Corps)** [KPA Ground Forces] (corps_3) — type: corps, HQ: Sariwon or Wonsan region (approximate), strength: 70.0%. Second-echelon conventional corps. Coastal/rear-area sector. Sub-unit composition unknown. [Wikipedia KPAGF; DIA NKMP 2021]
- **Former VI Corps (disbanded ~1996) — units absorbed by IX Corps** [KPA Ground Forces] (corps_6_note) — type: corps, HQ: N/A — disbanded, strength: 0.0%. VI Corps HQ was Chongjin, North Hamgyong Province. Disbanded ~1996 after corruption/bribery scandal involving corps staff and local officials per Bermudez 'Shield of the Great Leader'. Units (incl. 24th and 42nd Divisions) reallocated to IX Corps. [Wikipedia KPAGF citing Bermudez; DIA NKMP 2021 context] [POSSIBLY DISBANDED; confirm with current ROK DWP]
- **VII Corps (7th Corps)** [KPA Ground Forces] (corps_7) — type: corps, HQ: Hamhung or east-coast sector (approximate), strength: 68.0%. Second-echelon corps. Coastal sector responsibility. Sub-unit names unconfirmed. [Wikipedia KPAGF; DIA NKMP 2021]
- **VIII Corps (8th Corps)** [KPA Ground Forces] (corps_8) — type: corps, HQ: Approximate: north-central DPRK, strength: 65.0%. Conventional infantry corps; exact location and sub-unit composition not confirmed in open-source reporting. [DIA NKMP 2021 references 10 regular corps; FAS irp.fas.org]
- **IX Corps (9th Corps)** [KPA Ground Forces] (corps_9) — type: corps, HQ: North Hamgyong Province (approximate), strength: 65.0%. Absorbed 24th and 42nd Divisions from defunct VI Corps. Sub-unit details limited. [Wikipedia KPAGF; DIA NKMP 2021]
- **X Corps (10th Corps)** [KPA Ground Forces] (corps_10) — type: corps, HQ: Unknown; northern DPRK (approximate), strength: 62.0%. 10th of the conventional corps. Sub-unit names and HQ not confirmed in open sources. FAS 1996 document lists 8 conventional corps; number has since expanded. [DIA NKMP 2021 '10 regular corps'; FAS]
- **806th Mechanized Corps** [KPA Ground Forces] (corps_806) — type: mech_corps, HQ: Approximate: north of Pyongyang, along western axis LOC, strength: 75.0%. One of two mechanized exploitation corps. In 2003 reporting, the 806th and 815th Mechanised Corps formed the second operational echelon behind the Forward Corps. Equipped with Chonma-ho/Pokpung-ho variants. Sub-brigades unconfirmed. [Wikipedia KPAGF 2003 report; DIA NKMP 2021; IISS MB 2017]
- **815th Mechanized Corps** [KPA Ground Forces] (corps_815) — type: mech_corps, HQ: Approximate: eastern axis LOC, north of DMZ forward corps, strength: 73.0%. Second mechanized exploitation corps. 2003 reporting places it as part of second operational echelon. Some 2003 reports indicate a third mechanized corps in strategic reserve. Sub-brigades unconfirmed. [Wikipedia KPAGF; DIA NKMP 2021; IISS MB 2017]
- **820th Armored Corps** [KPA Ground Forces] (corps_820) — type: armor_corps, HQ: Sariwon, Hwanghae Province, strength: 70.0%. Armored exploitation corps. Mission: high-speed breakthrough to ROK rear. Contains 105th Armored Division + estimated armored/mech brigades. Main equipment: Chonma-ho (T-62 derivative). Only 'partially equipped' with Pokpung-ho per Mizokami 2020. ROK/US presume multiple armored and mechanized infantry brigades in addition to 105th Division. [Wikipedia KPAGF citing Mizokami/ROK-US assessment; DIA NKMP 2021]
- **620th Artillery Corps (Forward Artillery Corps)** [KPA Ground Forces] (corps_620) — type: arty_corps, HQ: Approximate: Kaesong region / forward area north of DMZ, strength: 88.0%. HIGHEST READINESS GROUND FORMATION. Operates the famed 170mm Koksan SP guns (range ~60km) and 240mm MRLs (range ~60km) that target Seoul. All systems in range of the Seoul metropolitan area population ~25M. DIA notes some reports that the 620th has been reorganised as '518th Artillery Division' — name/number transition unverified. [Wikipedia KPAGF; DIA NKMP 2021; IISS MB 2017; irp.fas.org]
- **Pyongyang Defense Command** [KPA Ground Forces] (pdc) — type: corps, HQ: Pyongyang, strength: 90.0%. REGIME PROTECTION FORCE. Distinct from the Capital Defense Command/91st Corps. Purpose: protect Kim family and Party leadership from internal coup or urban unrest. Corps-sized element; some estimates 300,000 personnel in combined Bodyguard Command and Pyongyang Defense structure. Top leadership replaced Jan 2026 per ROK MND annual assessment [POST-2021 UPDATE: Yonhap Jan 2026]. [DIA NKMP 2021; ROK MND 2025 assessment]
- **Capital Defense Command (91st Corps)** [KPA Ground Forces] (cdc) — type: corps, HQ: Pyongyang outskirts, strength: 85.0%. ELITE. Protects Pyongyang from external attack (distinct from Pyongyang Defense Command which is regime protection). Designated as 91st Capital Defense Corps in some ROK sources. Sub-brigade composition not confirmed in unclassified open sources. [ROK DWP 2022; factsanddetails.com citing ROK MoD]
