## Subordinate Division / Brigade / Flotillas
- **170mm Koksan SP Artillery Brigade (620th Corps element)** (elite) (bde_arty_620_koksan) — type: artillery, HQ: Forward position north of DMZ, strength: 90.0%. ELITE / HIGHEST READINESS. Operates 170mm M-1978 / M-1989 Koksan self-propelled guns. Range ~60km; covers northern Seoul metro area. ~500 Koksan guns in inventory (est). Specific brigade number unconfirmed. [DIA NKMP 2021; factsanddetails.com; IISS]
- **240mm MLRS Brigade (620th Corps element)** (elite) (bde_arty_620_240mrl) — type: mlrs, HQ: Forward position north of DMZ, strength: 90.0%. ELITE / HIGHEST READINESS. Operates M-1985/M-1991 240mm MRL (range ~60km). Together with Koksan guns, the 620th Corps represents the primary conventional threat to Seoul. ~500+ MRL tubes (est). Specific brigade number unconfirmed. [DIA NKMP 2021; IISS; ROK DWP 2022]
