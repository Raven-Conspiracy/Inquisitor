# Korean People's Army — General Staff Directorate: Korean People's Army Air Force (Konggun)

**ID**: KPAF
**Headquarters**: Pyongyang
**Commander (current)**: —
**Map color code**: #1a6e6e
**Notes**: ~110,000 personnel. ~570 combat aircraft, ~200 helicopters (2024 est.). Renamed from 'Air and Anti-Air Force' in April 2022. Operates 6 divisions: 1st ACC (NW, HQ Kaechon), 2nd ACC (E coast, HQ Toksan), 3rd ACC (DMZ, HQ Hwangju), 5th Transport Div (HQ Taechon), 6th Transport Div (HQ Sondok), 8th Training Div (HQ Orang). Russian MiG-29/Su-27 transfer reported 2024 per INDOPACOM CDR Paparo — not yet confirmed delivered [POST-2021 UPDATE: INDOPACOM 2024]. [DIA NKMP 2021; Wikipedia KPAF 2024]

## Subordinate Corps / Fleet / Air Division / Missile Bureaus
- **1st Air Combat Command (1st ACC) / 1st Air Combat Division** [KPAF] (kpaf_1acc) — type: air_division, HQ: Kaechon, South Pyongan Province, strength: 55.0%. Northwestern sector. HQ Kaechon Airfield (2,500m runway). Units include 35th Fighter Rgt (J-6/MiG-19 at Kaechon), 49th Fighter Rgt (MiG-17F at Panghyon), 24th Bomber Rgt (H-5/Il-28 at Uiju), 55th Attack Rgt (Su-25 at Sunchon), 57th Fighter Rgt (MiG-29 at Sunchon), 58th Fighter Rgt (MiG-23ML at Pukchang), 60th Fighter Rgt (MiG-21Bis at Pukchang), 36th Fighter Rgt (J-6 at Onchon). [Wikipedia KPAF; DIA NKMP 2021]
- **2nd Air Combat Command (2nd ACC) / 2nd Air Combat Division** [KPAF] (kpaf_2acc) — type: air_division, HQ: Toksan, East Coast, strength: 50.0%. East coast sector. Units include 56th Fighter Rgt (MiG-21PF/J-7/F-7 at Toksan), 25th Bomber Rgt (Il-28/H-5 at Changjin-Up), 46th Fighter Rgt (MiG-21PFM/F-5 at Wonsan), 66th Helicopter Rgt (Mi-14PL at Wonsan), 71st Fighter Rgt (MiG-21PFM at Kuum-ni), 72nd Fighter Rgt (MiG-21PFM at Hwangsuwon). [Wikipedia KPAF]
- **3rd Air Combat Command (3rd ACC) / 3rd Air Combat Division** [KPAF] (kpaf_3acc) — type: air_division, HQ: Hwangju, North Hwanghae Province, strength: 52.0%. DMZ sector air defense and combat. HQ at Chunghwa (Air Defense & Combat Command). Units include 4th Fighter-Bomber Rgt (MiG-17F/MiG-21 at Taetan), 32nd Fighter-Bomber Rgt (J-5/MiG-17 at Nuchon-ni), 33rd + 11th Fighter-Bomber Rgts (J-5/MiG-17F at Kwail), 50th Fighter Rgt (MiG-21PFM at Hwangju), 86th Attack Rgt (Q-5A at Koksan), 63rd Attack Helicopter Rgt (Mi-24D at Ayang-ni). [Wikipedia KPAF]
- **5th Transport Division** [KPAF] (kpaf_5td) — type: air_division, HQ: Taechon, North Pyongan Province, strength: 55.0%. Primary SOF infiltration lift and transport. HQ Taechon. Units include An-2 transport wings at Taechon, Kwaksan; helicopter wings (Mi-8T/Mi-26) at Pukchang East (65th Helo Rgt); MD-500 helicopters (64th Rgt at Pukchang East — 84 aircraft, illegally obtained 1980s via West German sanctions busting). An-2 Colt is primary SOF insertion platform (invisible to radar, cloth/wood construction). [DIA NKMP 2021; Wikipedia KPAF; FAS KPA Guide Part 3]
- **6th Transport Division** [KPAF] (kpaf_6td) — type: air_division, HQ: Sondok, South Hamgyong Province, strength: 52.0%. East-coast transport and helicopter support. An-2 transport units at Sondok, Yonpo, Manpo, Kuktong; Mi-4/Mi-8/Mi-17 helicopter wings at Kowon and Pakhon. [Wikipedia KPAF]
- **8th Training Division** [KPAF] (kpaf_8training) — type: air_division, HQ: Orang, North Hamgyong Province, strength: 60.0%. Air force training formation. HQ Orang. Sub-units: 41st Rgt (MiG-15UTI/J-2 at Orang), training regiment at Samiyon (F-5A), Kilchu helicopter training (Mi-2), Kimchaek Air Force Academy (Sungam-Chongjin, BT-6), Kyongsong Flight Officers School (BT-6). [Wikipedia KPAF]
