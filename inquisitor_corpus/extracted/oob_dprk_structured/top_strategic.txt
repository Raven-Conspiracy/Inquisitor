# Korean People's Army — General Staff Directorate: KPA Strategic Force / Missile General Bureau (Jeonnyakgun)

**ID**: STRATEGIC
**Headquarters**: Pyongyang
**Commander (current)**: —
**Map color code**: #6e4a1a
**Notes**: Service-level branch established 2012, directly subordinate to General Staff Department. Operates all ballistic missile units (SRBM through ICBM). Grew from 8 to 13 missile brigades 2018-2020. Post-2021 additions: Hwasong-17 ICBM (tested Mar 2022), Hwasong-18 solid-fuel ICBM (Apr 2023), Hwasong-19 (Oct 2024), Hwasong-16B IRBM (Apr 2024). >200 road-mobile launchers. KN-25 super-large MLRS treated as missile system. [POST-2021 UPDATE: Wikipedia Strategic Force 2024; DIA NKMP 2021 Appendix A]

## Subordinate Corps / Fleet / Air Division / Missile Bureaus
- **Strategic Force SRBM Brigade Group (Hwasong-5/6/9/KN-23/24/25)** [KPA Strategic Force] (strat_srbm_bde_group) — type: missile_bureau, HQ: Multiple operating bases; first belt 50-90km north of DMZ, strength: 90.0%. First belt of SRBM units. Estimated 500-600 Scud (Hwasong-5/6/9) missiles, ~40 TELs. New systems KN-23 (Iskander-class, 17+ launchers), KN-24 (ATACMS-class, 9+ launchers), KN-25 600mm SRMRL (30 tracked + 9 truck chassis = 216 rockets). Between 2018-2020 Strategic Force expanded from 8 to 13 brigades total. Brigade names/numbers not confirmed in open sources except operational base locations. [Wikipedia Strategic Force; CSIS Beyond Parallel Kal-gol 2020; DIA NKMP 2021 Appendix A]
- **Strategic Force MRBM/IRBM Brigade Group (Hwasong-7/12/Pukguksong-2)** [KPA Strategic Force] (strat_mrbm_bde_group) — type: missile_bureau, HQ: Second belt 90-120km north of DMZ; Panghyon, Sino-ri, Sangnam-ri, Riman-ri, strength: 88.0%. Second belt MRBM units. 200-300 Hwasong-7 (Nodong) missiles, 30 TELs; targeting US forces in Japan. Pukguksong-2 solid MRBM (7+ deployed, northern bases). Hwasong-9 (Scud-ER MRBM, 1000km). Sino-ri base houses regiment-sized Nodong unit. Individual brigade numbers not confirmed. [Wikipedia Strategic Force; DIA NKMP 2021]
- **Strategic Force ICBM Brigade Group (Hwasong-14/15/17/18/19)** [KPA Strategic Force] (strat_icbm_bde_group) — type: missile_bureau, HQ: Third belt / deep interior; Yusang-ni, Hoejung-ni, Riman-ri (largest base), Yongnim, strength: 88.0%. ICBM force. Hwasong-14 (10,000+km tested Jul 2017), Hwasong-15 (13,000km, Nov 2017), Hwasong-17 mod 1 (>13,000km, 11+ systems, tested Mar 2022), Hwasong-18 solid-fuel ICBM (Apr 2023, first solid ICBM), Hwasong-19 (Oct 2024, at least 15,000km) [POST-2021 UPDATE: Wikipedia Strategic Force 2024]. Brigade/unit designations not in open sources; DIA NKMP 2021 notes 200+ road-mobile launchers total force-wide. [DIA NKMP 2021; Wikipedia Strategic Force]
- **Strategic Force SLBM Unit (Pukguksong-series)** [KPA Strategic Force] (strat_slbm) — type: missile_bureau, HQ: Sinpo, South Hamgyong Province (Sinpo South Shipyard / Mayang sub base), strength: 70.0%. SLBM capability. 8.24 Yongung (Sinpo-class SSB, 1 x Pukguksong tube) + Hero Kim Kun Ok (multi-tube SSB, commissioned Sep 2023). Nuclear-powered SSBN announced Mar 2025 — construction stage only [POST-2021 UPDATE: Wikipedia KPN 2025]. Pukguksong-1/3/4/5 variants in inventory; -1 tested 2016, -3 tested Oct 2019, -4/-5 paraded 2021. Operational readiness of multi-tube SSBN unconfirmed. [Wikipedia KPN; DIA NKMP 2021]
