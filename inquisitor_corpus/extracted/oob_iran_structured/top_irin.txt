- **IRIN 5th Naval District — Jask (Indian Ocean)** [IRIN] (irin_nd5) — type: naval_district, HQ: Jask, Hormozgan, strength: 75%. Forward operating base for extended IO deployments. Supports IRIN blue-water presence. [DIA IMP 2019 p.27; note: some sources fold Jask into 2nd ND; listed separately here per IRIN reorganization reporting]
