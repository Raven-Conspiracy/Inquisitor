# Iranian Armed Forces — Service Branch: IRGC Ground Forces (IRGCGF)

**ID**: IRGC_GF
**Headquarters**: Tehran
**Commander (current)**: Brigadier General Mohammad Pakpur
**Map color code**: #5e1a1a
**Notes**: IRGC ground component; ~150,000 personnel. 31 provincial corps plus Tehran city corps. Primarily light infantry/commando with independent maneuver brigades. Elite Saberin units. Army Aviation (IRGCAA) formed 2016. [DIA IMP 2019 p.18] [POST-2024-STRIKES STATUS UNCERTAIN: General Pakpur killed in airstrike per JPost March 2026; successor unknown].
