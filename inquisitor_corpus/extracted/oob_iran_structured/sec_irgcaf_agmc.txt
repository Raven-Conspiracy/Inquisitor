# Iranian Armed Forces — Region / Provincial Corps / Base: Al-Ghadir Missile Command (AGMC)

**Unit ID**: irgcaf_agmc
**Service**: IRGC-AF
**Type**: missile_command
**Headquarters**: Tehran (dispersed underground launch sites nationwide)
**Parent**: IRGC Aerospace Force (IRGCASF / IRGC-AF) (irgc_af)
**Strength (% authorized)**: 70%
**Notes**: Primary ballistic missile operational command under IRGC-AF. Commander: BG Mahmoud Bagheri Kazemabad (per Iran Watch 2020). Coordinates all SRBM/MRBM/IRBM brigades. [Iran Watch IRGC-AF 2020] [DIA IMP 2019 p.21] [POST-2024-STRIKES STATUS UNCERTAIN: solid-fuel mixing equipment destroyed Oct 2024; Khojir complex struck; production degraded but mobile launchers unaffected]
