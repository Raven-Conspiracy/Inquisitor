Purpose: The purpose of this course is to provide recently assessed warrant officers with initial military training in Military Occupational Specialty (MOS) 131A (Field Artillery 
Targeting Technician). These entry level warrant officers will obtain general knowledge of target acquisition, intelligence support, and precision fires. They will obtain specific 
skills and in-depth knowledge in the areas of Field Artillery mission command systems, fire support, counter battery fires, the Army targeting process, and the military decision 
making process. The training and education in this course will provide graduates with the knowledge and skills to perform as an Assistant Counterfire Officer, Field Artillery 
Intelligence Officer, Counterfire Officer, and Targeting Officer within the maneuver brigade or Division Artillery (DIVARTY).
